Via Village

LOCATION: Vika, Oslo

ENVIRONMENTAL PROFILE: BREAAM-NOR Excellent, fossile free building site

OWNERS: Aspelin Ramm 50%, Storebrand Asset Management 50%

COMPLETION: 2021

The office and commercial building VIA is going to be an environmental, energy efficient and positive addition to the district of Vika. VIA is located in Oslos CBD and will house some of Norway's largest actors within banking, finance, law, real estate and consulting. The complex will be a combination of modern offices, flexible office space, restaurants, shopping and health treatments. VIA will be the gold standard for efficient energy management and sustainable solutions in the choice of materials, techniques, and design. Diagonal passages between the buildings create an open structure, that will stimulate social interaction in the area and give Vika new and attractive city spaces
